Hi! I'm Evelynne Lee

I am a rising fourth year student at the University of California San Diego where I study Cognitive Science with a specialization in Design and Interaction.

I believe that all good designs are designed with an intention for the users. I hope to apply human centered thinking to provide the best solutions to everyday problems. I am passionate about understanding the way things are designed and always wondering how it can be better.
