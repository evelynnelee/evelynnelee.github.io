Hi! I'm Evelynne Lee

I am currently a third year student at the University of California San Diego where I study Cognitive Science with a specialization in Design and Interaction.

I believe that all good designs are designed with an intention FOR the users. I hope to apply human centered thinking to provide the best solutions to everyday problems. I am passionate about understanding the way things are designed and always wondering how it can be better.

Some of my favorite things include visiting cute cafes to drink delicious lattes and learning all the new BTS songs to play on the piano. Interested in getting a coffee to chat more? Feel free to say hi!
